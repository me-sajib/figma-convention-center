About Our Center

Our Convention Centre (CC) is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2003 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry. Spectra Convention Centre offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.

Frequently Asked Questions

Open-flame candles are not allowed. If you would like the ambiance of candles for your event, they need to be LED. Special exceptions must be approved by your Event Manager. For more information on booth rules and regulations, visit our Planner's Guide page.

The typical daily parking rate is $15 for up to 16 hours; $21 a day for in-and-out privileges (subject to change). Due to demand and location, the fees vary between parking garages and events. Feel free to inquire about a specific event’s pricing.

To book a space, submit your event info through the form on the website. We will email you with more information on how to proceed. For information on venues or to book a room, visit our Floor Plans page.
